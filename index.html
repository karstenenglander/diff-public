Diff — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026 · Developed by Karsten Englander

In short: Diff does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything happens on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party services.

1. Information we collect

None. Diff does not collect or transmit any personal information. The app has no network connection and sends no data off your device.

2. Camera

Diff uses your device's camera to display and record a real-time visual effect. Camera frames are processed live on your device and are only written to a video if you choose to record and export one. Camera data is never transmitted anywhere.

3. Microphone

Diff uses your device's microphone to record audio as part of the videos you capture. Audio is recorded only while a recording is in progress, is written into the video file on your device, and is never transmitted anywhere. If you don't grant microphone access, recordings are simply saved without audio.

4. Photo library

Diff requests add-only access to your photo library for the sole purpose of saving videos you choose to export. Diff cannot read, browse, or access your existing photos or videos.

5. On-device settings

Your in-app preferences (effect parameters and similar settings) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. They never leave your device.

6. Analytics & tracking

Diff contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking technologies. It does not use the Advertising Identifier and does not track you across other apps or websites.

7. Third parties

Diff does not share data with third parties, because it does not collect any.

8. Children's privacy

Diff does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email diff@karstenenglander.com.
