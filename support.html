Diff — Support

Developed by Karsten Englander

Diff turns motion into a real-time visual effect, processed entirely on your device.

Need help? Email diff@karstenenglander.com and we'll get back to you.

Common questions

Permissions

Diff needs Camera access to show and record the effect, and add-only Photos access to save your exports. You can manage these any time in iOS Settings → Diff.

Recording

Tap the record button to start and stop. When you stop, your processed video appears for preview, where you can Save it to Photos or Share it.

Where are my videos saved?

Saved videos go to your Photos library, in a "Diff" album.

Privacy

Diff runs entirely on your device and collects no data. See the Privacy Policy for details.
